Tin Can Planter Pals

Materials:

  • Tin Cans (rinsed and dried)
  • Pipe Cleaners
  • Pom Poms
  • Googly Eyes
  • Scissors
  • Glue
  • Small Plants

Instructions:

  1. Glue the googly eyes on the tin can.
  2. Add a pom pom nose and a pip cleaner mouth.
  3. Allow the glue to dry, and add a small plant to create an adorable planter!
