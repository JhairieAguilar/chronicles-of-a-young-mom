Advice for College Students

As college students, we tend to hear the same advice over and over again, from the importance of going to office hours to the need for extracurriculars. But we never get the chance to hear the things that we actually do want to hear. Well here it is:

  1. Don't be afraid to take time off.
  2. Take advantage of this time to become the most amazin version of yourself.
  3. Understand that the biggest cause of roomate conflicts is a failure to communicate.
  4. Build yourself, not your resume.
  5. Don't wait to figure out your life after college-start now.
  6. Make time for yourself
  7. Spend time learning how to communicate.
  8. Don't be proud of your college-make your college proud to have you.
