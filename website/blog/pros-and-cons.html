The Pros And Cons Of Being A Young Mom

Pros:

  1. You have so much more energy before you reach the age of 30.
  2. Your skin is more stretchy when you are young so it is more likely to repair itself to its pre-pregnancy state.
  3. Being constantly reminded of how young you are is quite nice to hear when you reach your thirties.
  4. You have more chances of lioving to see your great, great, great grandchildren!
  5. You will still be young when your kids have grown up
  6. You will identify better with your teenagers

Cons:

  1. Education and career may be put on hold.
  2. Losing your figure earlier
  3. You are limited in the amount of "fun" things you can do.
  4. There may be financial challenges
  5. At times there may be no life for self
  6. Health complications
