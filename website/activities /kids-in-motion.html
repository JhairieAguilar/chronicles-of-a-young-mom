Kids in Motion

Kids in Motion offers four to seven hours of free activities, including organized sports, games, and fitness demos, in some parks and playgrounds. Children are introduced to physical activities in a fun, noncompetitive environment. Kids in Motions helps them become more active through adulthood.

Joining is FREE! and no registration is required. To find out the playgrounds where this is held and for their hours of operation, head over to www.nycgovparks.org
